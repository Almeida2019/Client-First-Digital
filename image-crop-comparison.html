Mobile Image Cropping Fix: Before vs After

❌ BEFORE (Problematic)
Problem:
• object-position: center top
• Cuts off person's head on mobile
• Shows too much lower body
• Poor user experience
✅ AFTER (Fixed)
Solution:
• object-position: center 15%
• Keeps person's head visible
• Shows laptop and work area
• Much better mobile experience

✅ Problem Solved!

The key fix was changing from object-position: center top to center 15%

This ensures that on mobile devices, the image crops to show the most important parts (faces, laptops, work areas) instead of cutting off heads and showing empty space at the bottom.

Resize your browser window to see the mobile behavior
